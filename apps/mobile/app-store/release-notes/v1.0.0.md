## English

### Welcome to D Block Workspace!

We are excited to launch the official D Block Workspace app -- your all-in-one tool for managing your coworking experience at D Block locations in Tbilisi and Batumi.

### What's New in v1.0.0

#### Space Booking
- Book meeting rooms, hot desks, private offices, phone booths, parking spots, lockers, and event spaces
- Real-time availability across all D Block locations
- View space details, amenities, and capacity before booking
- Manage, modify, or cancel your upcoming reservations

#### Coworking Passes
- Purchase hourly, daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, or annual passes
- Seamless in-app payment processing via Bank of Georgia and TBC Bank
- Auto-renewal options for monthly, quarterly, and annual passes

#### Smart Door Access
- Unlock doors at D Block locations using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE)
- Powered by SaltoKS integration for secure, keyless access
- Works across all locations: Stamba Hotel, Radio City, and Rooms Hotel Batumi

#### B2B Company Management
- Create and manage company accounts
- Add, remove, and manage team member access
- Track employee bookings and usage
- Centralized billing for organizations

#### Billing & Invoices
- View and download invoices
- Track payment history
- Pay outstanding invoices directly from the app
- GEL currency support with BOG/TBC payment processing

#### Visitor Registration
- Register visitors and generate guest passes
- Share access details with your guests
- Manage active visitor passes

#### Credits & Promotions
- Earn credits through the referral program
- Apply promo codes at checkout
- Track your credit balance and transaction history

#### Multi-Location Support
- Stamba Hotel, 14 Merab Kostava St, Tbilisi
- Radio City, 3 Akhvlediani Ascent, Tbilisi
- Rooms Hotel Batumi, 1 Ninoshvili St, Batumi

#### Language Support
- English
- Georgian (ქართული)
